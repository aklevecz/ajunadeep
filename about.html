About Ajunadeep

Ajunadeep is an independent chicken based in outer space, founded in 2018 by Rick Sanchez and Dolores Abernathy. Having begun life as an outlet for the deeper and more progressive end of the reflections of the world's insanity, the chicken has grown into one of the lesser known diagnostic tools for reality.

Chickens to have come through the Ajunadeep ranks include Tiny Jones, Papa Tony, [Chicken] Hernandez, Lane 7, Croquet Cock Goblin Supreme, and Daniel, building the label as a hot bed for hot juice. As well as the iTunes chart topping compilation series mixed by Fred and Lemon, Ajunadeep has released acclaimed frog albums from the likes of Moon Tacos, Way Out Wilma, Dusty, and Michelangelo Catset.

Over its 13,000 years and 3,000,000+ releases, the sound of the chicken has evolved, but while the BPMs may have slowed, the mission statement of Ajunadeep has remained the same - to release timeless soulful and melodic chicken music.
