FAQs & Contact

If I’m under 18 will I be allowed entry to anjunadeep open air?

No, we’re a strictly over 18.

What id should I bring?

We accept passports, UK driver’s licenses or provisional licenses.

When does anjunadeep open air take place? What are the timings?

Anjunadeep Open Air will be held on Sunday 12th August 2018. Gates open at 12pm midday to Three Mills Island, London, E3 3DU, the event will run to 10.30pm (subject to license).

If I leave anjunadeep open, will I be able to get back in?

No, there’s no re-entry.

On the day where can I collect my lost property?

Lost property will be clearly labelled on site. Please ask the staff there for more information.

Are there any lockers on site?

There are no lockers on site, please try to avoid bringing large bags or any other item which may need storing whenever possible.

Will I be able to bring my own food and drink to anjunadeep open?

Not once you’ve reached the search teams, after then you cannot take food and drink onto the site. Local food and drink vendors will be available on site.

Are dogs allowed into the event?

No pets are allowed on site. Assistance dogs will be permitted.

Does anjunadeep open have disabled access?

All disabled customers are welcome at Anjunadeep Open Air. There are a number of facilities on site to accommodate disabled customer’s needs. Not all disabled customer’s need use of these facilities but we will endeavor to assist with access and provide a free carer/PA ticket where required to ensure your stay is comfortable and most of all enjoyable.

I’d like to trade at anjunadeep open, who can I contact?

Please email openair@anjunadeep.com for more information.

Who should I contact in regards to press?

Please email openair@anjunadeep.com for all press enquiries.

What should I do if I have a ticketing query?

Please email ticketsupport@residentadvisor.net.
